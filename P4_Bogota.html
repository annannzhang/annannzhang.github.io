Planning Studio

Urban Expansion and Equity in Bogota: Examining Housing & Transportation Disparities

Fall 2023 | Instructed by Professor Erick Guerra
With Alexa Ringer, Brianna Thornhill, Elaine Zhang, Jared Jackson, Ke Zhou, Michael Dunst, Naomi Tariku, Teresa Chang, Xiayuanshan Gao, & Yanbing (Evan) Zhao



This planning studio project brings students from University of Pennsylvania in Philadelphia and Universidad de Los Andes in Bogota together in examining housing and transportation disparities in the city of Bogota and then proposing potential interventions. The full report is a collaborative effort from my teammates and me, and it encapsulates urban context, framework of analysis, and four key interventions: 1) Inclusive Futures, 2) Maximizing the Metro (a TOD proposal centering around the new Metro system), 3) Low-Emission Corridors (utilitzing the famous existing BRT corridor) and 4) Safety Improvements (both physical enhancements and sysmetic upgrades).
